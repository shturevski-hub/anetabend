ANETA LJUMAKOVSKA

TECHNIQUEATMOSPHERE

Aneta Ljumakovska was born on 25.07.1983 in Bitola and is a famous Macedonian singer and one of the most recognizable performers of folk and newly composed music in Macedonia and the region. ​She graduated from a technical high school in her hometown. From a young age, she showed a strong interest and talent for music. ​With her specific voice, emotional interpretation and authentic style, Aneta quickly won the attention of the audience and received countless awards at festivals both in Macedonia and in the neighboring countries. ​As a former member of "Molika", she has performed at numerous concerts, festivals, and cultural events, both in the country and in the diaspora. During her career, she has performed a number of hits that are well received by the audience and are often part of the Macedonian folk scene. ​Aneta is now part of the Aneta Band together with some of the members of "Molika" and is appreciated for her professionalism, modesty and dedication to music, and her performances are recognizable by the strong emotion and contact with the audience.

STIV SHTUREVSKI

WRITTERBACK VOCAL

Steve Sturevski, Macedonian singer, composer, arranger and actor. ​Born on 22-1-1975 in Ohrid, Republic of Macedonia. ​Completed secondary technical education. ​Music mentoring education. He comes from a large musical family, so from the youngest age he is completely devoted to music as a harmonica player, synthesizer and mainly vocals. ​He was a member of many Macedonian music groups, such as "Group Fontana", "Group Molika". ​After a long career, he temporarily retired from the profession in 2016. ​Now again to be an active member of one of the best Macedonian folk groups "Aneta Band". ​He has performed all over the world, including America, Australia, Canada and most of Europe. ​He is known for the music hits "Ajshe", "Lichno Nade", "Sela Pelisterski" and many others. ​He achieved great popularity and success in 2012 with the recording of the musical comedy "The Road to the Kangaroos", he also appeared in 2020 in the film "Enemy". ​Steve has also been decorated with various awards, such as the "Oscar of Popularity" 2010, 2011, 2012. ​Currently his role is Back Vocal or Accompanying Vocal and Accompanying Instruments.

RUBIN RAZMOVSKI

PRECISIONRHYTHM

Rubin Razmoski was born on 19.09.1982 in Prilep, where he showed his love and talent for music from a young age. ​He is a prominent Macedonian musician who is recognizable by his wind instruments with which he began to cultivate the tradition as a young member of KUD Mirce Acev from Prilep, and his style has a significant influence on the recognizable sound of Macedonian music. With many years of dedication, Razmoski has established himself as a top instrumentalist, known for his technical skills, sense of rhythm and authentic interpretation of Macedonian folk and newly composed music. As a former member of "Molika", Rubin Razmoski has performed at numerous concerts, festivals, cultural events and private events, both in Macedonia and abroad. ​As a member of Aneta Band, his role in the group is crucial, both on stage and behind it. Rubin Razmoski is valued among colleagues and audiences as a professional, innovative musician and guardian of the Macedonian musical tradition, who successfully combines tradition with contemporary sound.

DRUMS

ALEKSANDAR (KARMATA) TALEVSKI

RHYTHMENERGY

Alexander Talevski Karma Aleksandar Talevski Karma is a Macedonian musician from Bitola, born on May 13, 1981. From an early age, he shows a great love for music, and he begins his musical journey at only 14 years old, when he starts performing in local restaurants and events. After finishing secondary technical school in Bitola, he devoted himself completely to music. His specialty is percussion instruments – drums, tarabuka and village drum, through which he creates a recognizable and authentic rhythm. His professional career began with membership in the group „Macedonian Voice“, and later continued in the group „Dynamics“. He spends the most significant part of his career in the legendary group „Molika“, where he has been an active member since 2007, for almost 17 years. With this group, he performs in many countries around the world, conveying Macedonian song and music with his masterful rhythm. During his career, Alexander performed in Australia, the USA, Canada and throughout Europe, and with his colleagues they won numerous awards and recognitions. As part of their achievements, they also made the musical film „The Road to Kangaroos“, which is a significant project in their artistic work. Today he is an active member of „Aneta Band“, where he continues to create and enrich the Macedonian music scene with his talent and experience.

ZISO PUROVSKI

MELODYATMOSPHERE

Ziso Purovski was born in 1975 in Bitola. He completed his secondary education in 1994 at the secondary music school in Bitola. He graduated from the Faculty of Music in 1998 in Skopje, and completed his master's studies in 2003. As cellist (member (and one of the founders ) he performed with the contemporary music ensemble “Alea”. He attended several seminars and master courses with Anatoly Krstev, Kiril Rodin, etc. He attended several master courses in contemporary music! The founder of the string quartet “ARKO” and with it he performed numerous performances both in the country and abroad (Europe, Ukraine, Serbia, Greece Macedonia, leaving behind numerous successful concerts, festivals and solo performances. He currently works as a full professor of cello at the secondary music school in Bitola.

VANGEL MAZHEVSKI

PROFESSIONALPRECISE

Born on January 20, 1986, in Bitola, R. Macedonia Education: He started his secondary music education at the State Music School – Bitola in 2000, majoring in theory. After finishing high school, he went to Salzburg, where he took the entrance exams in composition at the Mozart Conservatory, from where he returned to Macedonia for justified reasons. After a two-year break, he continues his higher education, undergraduate studies at the University of St. „Kiril and Metodij“ – at the Faculty of Music in Skopje, Department of Music Theory and Pedagogy. He has exceptional talent, and he especially stands out for his professional theoretical subjects, compositional and improvisational abilities, piano, etc. Since his high school days until today, he has been professionally engaged in composing and orchestrating/arranging. He graduated in 2011, in which he enrolled in a master's degree in the subject “Harmony with harmonic analysis” under the mentorship of Zivoin Glisic. Since August 2012, – has been the founder of the Jazz Screenplay lineup as an art director, composer, arranger and pianist. In 2012, he started teaching in theoretical professional subjects at DMU „Todor Skalovski – Tetoec“, and after a short period of 2013 at the State Music School in Bitola as a professor of harmony, composition, contact solfege and piano. In 2015, he enrolled in undergraduate studies at the Faculty of Musical Arts in Skopje in the field of Orchestral Conducting with Professor Sasha Nikolovski – Gjumar. In 2017, through the Erasmus Plus exchange program, he listened to a dig at the Music Academy in Ljubljana, Slovenia, with professors Marko Letonja, Marko Hibernik and Simon Dvorsak. He is professionally engaged in audio production, mixing and mastering. For many years he has been collaborating with the Bitola Chamber Orchestra as an arranger and performer. He is a participant in several state and international competitions as a jury committee, including Piano Fest – Bitola as a jury committee for chamber music. At the beginning of 2024, he collaborated with Piano Trio Trifonika, who, within 77 years of the establishment of the State Music School – Bitola, premiered his composition “Variations on the theme Macedonian girl” for piano for 6 hands, which was specially composed and dedicated to Trio Trifonika. Awards: 2003 – Solfeggio – first prize 2009 – Solfeggio – Special Award

JANE DINEV

ATMOSPHEREPARTY

The Macedonian singer Jane Dinev was born on March 8, 1999 in Kavadarci. From an early age, the environment recognized his musical talent and special charisma.He started his musical journey at the age of 18 when he performed at various events and private parties, and the wider audience got to know him through performances on music television shows. He is currently building his musical career as a singer, his musicality is complemented by knowledge of accordion and synthesizer. He has appeared on stage with various musicians but also as a singer in the music groups Clasico Band and Galaxy Band. Jane Dinev is considered one of the young musicians with great potential on the Macedonian scene and he continues his musical work as a member of Aneta Band, with which he continues to develop his musical career.
