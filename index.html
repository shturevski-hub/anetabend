ABOUT THE BAND

Based in Bitola, North Macedonia, Aneta Band represents the pinnacle of modern Balkan music fusion. Led by the powerhouse vocals of Aneta Ljumakovska, the ensemble is celebrated for blending soulful traditions with contemporary musical excellence.

With millions of views on YouTube and a legacy of performances across Europe, Australia, and North America, the band remains at the forefront of the Macedonian music scene, bridging cultural heritage with world-class performance.

KEY HIGHLIGHTS

  • • Over 15 years of professional musical experience.
  • • Global tours including Europe, Australia, and USA.
  • • Millions of collective streams on official platforms.
  • • Specialized in premium wedding and concert entertainment.
