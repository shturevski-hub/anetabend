BIOGRAPHY

Aneta Bend is a world-renowned musical ensemble led by the powerhouse vocals of Aneta Ljumakovska. Based in Bitola, North Macedonia, the band is celebrated for blending soulful Balkan traditions with contemporary musical excellence. With millions of views on YouTube and a legacy of performances across Europe, Australia, and North America, Aneta Bend remains at the forefront of the Macedonian music scene.
